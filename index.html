FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is EditPDFDoc really free?
Yes! All 20+ PDF tools on EditPDFDoc are completely free. The free plan has 2 tasks/hour and files up to 200 MB.
Are my uploaded files secure?
Absolutely. All uploads are SSL encrypted. Files auto-delete after 2 hours. We never read or share your documents.
Do I need to install any software?
No installation required. EditPDFDoc works entirely in your browser on any device.
What file size is supported?
Free: up to 200 MB. Pro: up to 2 GB. Business: unlimited.
Is there an API available?
Yes. The EditPDFDoc REST API is available on the Business plan. See /api/health and /api/system for details. editpdfdoc.com
