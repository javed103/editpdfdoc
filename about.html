# About Us Welcome to the editpdfdoc project! The editpdfdoc repository is designed to provide users with efficient tools for editing PDF documents. Whether you're looking to annotate, merge, split, or convert PDF files, our project aims to make the process simple and user-friendly. ## Our Mission We strive to help individuals and organizations manage their PDF documents seamlessly. With a focus on usability and performance, our tools are crafted to fulfill the needs of our users, no matter their technical expertise. ## Features - **Annotation Tools**: Highlight, comment, and mark up PDF files. - **Merge and Split**: Combine multiple PDFs into one file or break a PDF into smaller parts. - **Conversion Options**: Convert PDFs to various formats and vice versa. ## Get Involved We welcome contributions from anyone motivated to improve the project. Feel free to raise issues, suggest features, or submit pull requests. Thank you for using editpdfdoc!
